## Overview

Every field has its dogmas — the confident claims that get repeated until they feel like facts. Pushback Jack is a video series dedicated to poking holes in them.

Not to be contrarian. To be honest.

## What It Is

Pushback Jack is an animated character who delivers short, punchy rebuttals to overconfident claims — in AI, business, culture, wherever the BS is thick enough to deserve it.

Each episode takes a handful of real, widely-repeated assertions and gives them the scrutiny they deserve. The format is simple: claim, pushback, next. No long essays. No hedging. Just a lumberjack with a point of view and a stack of index cards.

Episode one took on the AI hype cycle — the claims that AI will replace coders, that it's already smarter than humans, that every company needs an AI strategy or they'll be left behind. Jack had thoughts.

## The Show

Produced at Lumberjack Studios. Built with [Remotion](https://remotion.dev) for the text compositing and [CreateStudio](https://creatingstudio.co) for Jack himself. The workflow is part of the project — an AI-assisted production pipeline that keeps episodes fast to produce without sacrificing the craft.
